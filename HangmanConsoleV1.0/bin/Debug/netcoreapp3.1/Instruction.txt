-- Tutorial --
--------------
1. When you choose to play, you will be given sequence of word.
2. Player will be given 8 chances to guess at the given word.
3. Every wrong character guessed, player will lose 1 chance to guess.
4. Should one player lost all chances to guess, they lost. Otherwise, player continue to another level.
5. At the end of the game, player will be asked to write their name and their score will be shown.
